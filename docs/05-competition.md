# Competition

## Summary

The broad market is crowded. The narrow wedge is not.

Do not compete as a generic stablecoin processor. Compete as a Sui-native, gasless service escrow paylink.

## Competitor layers

| Layer | Examples | Competition level | Enter? |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Web2 payments | Stripe, PayPal, Wise, Airwallex | Very high | No |
| Generic stablecoin payments | Coinbase Payments, Sphere, Loop | Very high | No |
| Crypto checkout/paylinks | Coinbase Commerce, Helio, SmoothSend | High | No broad entry |
| Crypto invoicing | Request Finance, InvoiceNow, Reap | High | No broad entry |
| Sui gas infrastructure | Enoki, Shinami, Ignitia | Medium-high | No infra entry |
| Sui gasless stablecoin service escrow | Narrow emerging space | Lower | Yes |

## Differentiation

SuiPayLink differentiates by combining:

- Sui gasless stablecoin transfer narrative.
- Sponsored transaction UX for escrow operations.
- Service-specific escrow flow.
- Simple paylink UX.
- On-chain receipt object.

## Main substitutes

### Direct stablecoin transfer

Fastest substitute. We beat it only when escrow, receipt, and buyer/seller guidance matter.

### PayPal goods and services

Better for mainstream buyers. We beat it only for stablecoin-native cross-border users who dislike account holds or need on-chain settlement.

### Request Finance

Better for B2B crypto invoicing and finance ops. We beat it only by being lighter, Sui-native, and service escrow-focused.

### Helio / Coinbase Commerce

Better general crypto checkout. We beat them only on Sui gasless UX and escrow service transaction focus.

## Competitive claim

Narrow claim:

> SuiPayLink is the simplest way to turn a cross-border digital service agreement into a gasless Sui stablecoin escrow link.

Avoid broad claim:

> SuiPayLink is the best crypto payment processor.

## Risk

If Sui Foundation or a wallet ships generic payment links, SuiPayLink must stay vertical:

- service escrow templates;
- contributor bounty payout;
- AI service payment workflow;
- delivery proof and receipt management.
