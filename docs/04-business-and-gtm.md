# Business And GTM

## Positioning

SuiPayLink is not a generic checkout. It is:

> Gasless stablecoin escrow links for cross-border digital service work.

## Who pays

Primary payer:

- Seller/service provider who wants easier stablecoin collection and escrow.

Secondary payer:

- Web3 project team that wants to pay contributors, creators, translators, or contractors.

## Why they pay now

- Cross-border payment friction is already painful.
- Stablecoin users dislike explaining gas and wallets to buyers.
- Service transactions need trust and proof of delivery.
- Sui just made gasless stablecoin payments a current ecosystem narrative.

## First commercial offer

Do not sell SaaS first. Sell setup service first.

Offer:

> We set up a branded Sui stablecoin escrow paylink workflow for your next contributor, AI service, or bounty payout.

Price:

- Pilot setup: $300-$1,000.
- Sui project/team setup: $1,000-$5,000.
- Platform fee later: 0.5%-1.5% of escrowed volume.

## Early ICPs

1. Sui ecosystem teams paying contributors.
2. Web3 content/translation/tutorial buyers.
3. AI automation consultants accepting stablecoins.
4. Small agencies doing cross-border work.
5. Hackathon teams paying bounties or freelancers.

## Why not Web2 payment tools

Web2 wins domestic card checkout. SuiPayLink wins when:

- the buyer or seller wants stablecoin settlement;
- a merchant account is unavailable or slow;
- payment is cross-border;
- escrow and proof of delivery matter;
- the user should not manage SUI gas;
- a chain-verifiable receipt is useful.

## Revenue streams

### Near term

- Done-for-you setup.
- White-label paylink page.
- Escrow workflow setup for Sui teams.

### Productized

- Transaction fee on escrow.
- Monthly seller plan.
- Team plan with branded pages and exports.
- API access for project teams.

## Operating plan after hackathon

1. Use the hackathon demo to approach Sui ecosystem projects.
2. Offer three free pilots in exchange for feedback and public demo permission.
3. Convert successful pilots into paid setup.
4. Measure actual payment volume and repeat usage.
5. Build only the features repeated users request.

## Metrics

- Created paylinks.
- Funded paylinks.
- Escrow release rate.
- Total stablecoin volume.
- Repeat sellers.
- Sponsor gas cost as percentage of fee revenue.
- Average time from link creation to payment.

## Kill criteria

Kill commercial push if:

- potential users prefer direct wallet transfer and do not value escrow;
- buyers cannot easily access Sui stablecoins;
- sponsor gas cost or operational risk exceeds fee revenue;
- users require fiat rails before they will use it;
- no Sui ecosystem team agrees to test after targeted outreach.
